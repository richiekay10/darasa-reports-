How It Works

Darasa Reports integrates seamlessly with your existing school systems. Here’s how to get started:

1. Setup

Sign up and configure Darasa Reports for your school. The process is quick, with guided steps to input school information.

2. Import Data

Import student and teacher data from your existing databases or input them manually. Our system is compatible with various formats.

3. Manage & Track

Begin managing student information, tracking grades, and generating reports. Our support team is here to help if needed.
